## Requirements Overview
Transform Nolimit360 (design subscription service) into Nolimit Designs - a professional web development agency serving Edmonton and surrounding areas. Create SEO-friendly pages, industry portfolios for 20 industries, location pages, blog content, lead generation system, and 3 demo mini-websites.

## User Stories
- As a potential client, I can browse industry-specific portfolio pages to see relevant web design examples
- As a visitor, I can find the agency through location-based SEO pages (Edmonton, St. Albert, etc.)
- As a lead, I can contact the agency via form, phone, email, chat widget, or booking calendar
- As a visitor, I can view demo websites showcasing the agency's capabilities
